Presenting PyConZA 2012 Sponsorship

PyConZA is the very first gathering in South Africa for the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. PyConZA is organized by the Python community for the community and presented by Praekelt Foundation. Our aim is to make PyConZA accessible to the widest group possible and to foster unique solutions to the challenges we face in Africa. The conference is intended to draw diverse participants to an enjoyable experience in the Mother City, Cape Town, South Africa.

Your sponsorship will not only help launch PyconZA in its inaugural year, but will keep it affordable and accessible to the widest possible audience. The available sponsorships are described below. If you have suggestions please do not hesitate to contact us as we are willing to work with you to design the sponsorship package that will fulfill your business and organization's needs.
