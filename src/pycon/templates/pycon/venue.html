Venue Information

The Pavilion Clock Tower is situated within the Clock Tower in Cape Town's Victoria & Alfred Waterfront, boasting venues with stunning views of the harbour and the perfect place for our first PyConZA conference. We love this new venue and its green policy and PyConZA will be working closely with the venue to ensure that our event has the least impact on the environment possible.
